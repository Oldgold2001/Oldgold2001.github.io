About Me

I graduated from high school in 1996 from Centralia High School in Centralia, MO. Following high school, I attended the University of Missouri – Columbia and graduated in May of 2001 with a Bachelor of Science in Education with an emphasis in Secondary Social Studies. In 2006 I received my Masters in Educational Administration from Williams Woods University. Currently, I am working toward my Educational Specialist degree from the University of Missouri - Columbia with an emphasis in Learning Technologies and Development.
I have taught a variety of Social Studies courses over my 20-year career, including US Government for the past 6 years. The last 4 years at my current school I have helped develop and implement the Virtual US Government course. I have been involved in the formulating of activities and materials to teach the US Government curriculum through and online platform.
