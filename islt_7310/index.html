Landmark US Supreme Court Cases

Welcome

Welcome to the Landmark US Supreme Court website for PHS Virtual Government. It is important for everyone to understand the right and liberties we have as citizens of the United States. This website should help you better understand the impact of Judicial Review on the rights of US Citizens. We will do this by looking at 11 important US Supreme Court cases.

About the Site

Judicial Review is an important U.S. Government concept to understand. It is through this process the Judicial Branch can check the Legislative and Executive branches of the government. Judicial Review can also help to clarify rights granted to citizens and set precedent for lower courts. The purpose of this website is to give students a way to learn about Judicial review by looking at 11 important U.S. Supreme Court cases. The website contains Educator Credential as well as a Contact page to ask further questions. The main portion of the page is the Learning Overview. The Learning section contains 3 goals that look at Judicial Review, the main issue of each case, and then how the decision in each case connects to the U.S. Constitution.
